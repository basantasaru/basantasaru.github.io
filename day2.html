My Diary of November 2

Today my village brother went home at 5 AM, and I woke up at 8 AM. My neighbour’s little brother came to wake me up to cut his hair. I cut his hair, then came home and ate chowchow. I changed my clothes because I had to do heavy work, even though my body still wanted rest.

I start doing that work with dad, doing the task called — beating wet rice bundles on the ground to separate the grain. The rice was wet and heavy, so it was hard work. My mom worked too. After a short break around 10 AM and taking morning meal, we continued because it might rain, and wet rice bundles need to be finished quickly. We worked until around 4 PM, then I took a shower and rested in my room.

Later, I helped my mom cook rice and vegetables, and I went to buy a recharge card for my Namaste SIM. Unfortunately, the card cost Rs. 199, and I only had Rs. 198. I called two friends, and one friend sent Rs. 20 top-up to my SIM.

After that, I watched a video on YouTube about Lord Krishna is the unlimited truth, then I go for eating, while eating, I watched a video about Swami Vivekananda by Gaurav Thakur. I returned to my room and started research for my upcoming books. About Hinduism, while doing research I got chance to learn more. I learned today about Tridevi and Lord Shiva Bhairav’s 8 forms.

Today, I chanted “Shambh Sadha Shiv” more than any other day. I feel like by chanting I get energy to do work more, and I also used my phone very little. Overall, my day went well.

What I Learned Today

  • Teamwork makes tough tasks easier – Working with my dad and mom helped complete difficult tasks.
  • Small help matters – Even Rs. 20 top-up from a friend solved my problem, reminding me the value of friends and support.
  • Learning comes everywhere – While researching for my books, I learned more about Tridevi and Lord Shiva Bhairav’s 8 forms.
  • Spiritual energy is real – Chanting “Shambh Sadha Shiv” gave me energy to work and focus more.
  • Digital discipline helps – Using my phone very little today made me more focused and productive.
