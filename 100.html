About The Book

"100 Lessons for Life" shares timeless teachings from Hindu philosophy applied to everyday life. Learn how dharma, discipline, self-awareness, silence, and mindful decision-making can transform your habits, relationships, and inner strength across a lifetime.

Key Lessons

  • Dharma vs Comfort: Choosing the Right Path
  • Silence as a Form of Wisdom
  • Discipline as Inner Power
  • Raising Strong and Mindful Children
  • Awareness and Presence in Daily Life
  • Mastering Desire and Attachment
  • Balance Between Action and Rest
  • Financial Wisdom and Saving Habits
  • Understanding the Self Through Koshas
  • Reflection, Meditation, and Inner Growth
