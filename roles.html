The Three Forces of Existence

Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva in Everyday Life

About The Book

"The Three Forces of Existence" explores the cosmic roles of Brahma (Creation), Vishnu (Preservation), and Shiva (Transformation) and how their principles apply to daily life. Learn to align your actions with creation, balance, and change, and discover practical ways to live with purpose, discipline, and inner harmony.

Key Lessons

  • Brahma: Embracing Creation and New Beginnings
  • Vishnu: Maintaining Balance and Sustaining Life
  • Shiva: Harnessing Change and Transformation
  • Integrating Cosmic Principles in Daily Decisions
  • Discipline, Focus, and Self-Mastery
  • Overcoming Resistance to Change
  • Practical Lessons from the Trinity
  • Creating Harmony in Relationships and Work
  • Awareness of Life’s Cycles and Timing
  • Living with Purpose, Strength, and Clarity
